the program

Three to four artists. One month each.

Three to four artists per year. One month each in Puerto Escondido. Rent covered, meals handled, a per diem for everything else. You make work. You leave one piece behind that lives at Contorno.

That's it. That's the program.

what you get

included · the residency

The residency covers what most don't, so you can spend a month doing what you're here to do.

  1. A private unit at Contorno with La Punta views
  2. Meals at Tea & Sol, on us
  3. Per diem for everything else
  4. A studio space and the run of the property
  5. A month of uninterrupted time
  6. Your work shown, your story told, your audience grown

what we ask

One piece. Made here, for here.

Something that becomes part of Contorno after you've gone.

You cover your flight to PXM. We handle the rest.

who it's for

Artists with a body of work and a point of view.

Painters, photographers, sculptors, filmmakers, musicians, writers, anyone whose practice could use a month somewhere unfamiliar.

We're not looking for the most credentialed artist. We're looking for the one whose work we can't stop thinking about.

the space

Puerto Escondido · La Punta

Polished concrete, jungle palms, a gym that doesn't suck, and the Pacific 200 meters away. Designed by an architect who fell in love with this place. Built for makers.

questions, answered

No.

Can I bring a partner? +
Yes. Note it in the application.
What if I don't know what I'd make for the space yet? +
That's fine. We want to see how you think, not a finished pitch.
